Findings of Fact
  • Tim Cheese was present at the scene of death. His cheddar crumb and mouse-sized footprint confirm this. He denied owning a phone. A phone was vibrating in his pocket during questioning.
  • John Pork's last decoded message — "I KNOW WHAT YOU ATE" — was sent to Tim Cheese's number 11 minutes before death. Tim Cheese did not answer.
  • Tim Cheese lied under oath when asked about phone ownership. This constitutes perjury and implies prior knowledge of the events of April 14, 2023.
  • The Crow was present on the roof. The Crow stated it "saw nothing." Polygraph evidence indicates deception. The Crow was a witness who chose silence.
  • The 48th Dave attempted to warn John Pork 47 times via voicemail. John Pork did not receive the warning. This is because he was busy calling others who did not answer.
  • John Pork placed 47 calls on the night of April 14, 2023. Not one was answered. The clinical cause of death was "unanswered call failure." The actual cause of death was Tim Cheese.
Sentence

Tim Cheese is found guilty of one count of Deliberate Non-Response, one count of Cheese Concealment, and one count of Being Where He Should Not Have Been. He is sentenced to answer every call he receives for the rest of his natural life. He is also forbidden from eating cheddar in proximity to any memorial, monument, or chalk outline.

The Crow is found guilty as an accessory. Sentence: it must answer the phone once, for any caller, at least once per year. It knows why.

Signed in absentia —
The 47th and 48th Dave, collectively
