THE ABANDONED OFFICE

everything here is almost normal. this is the most frightening thing so far.

Level 4 is an office. Drop ceilings, carpet (drier than Level 0 — a mercy), cubicle walls in beige and a slightly different beige. The lights are fluorescent. The lights work. Everything in Level 4 works, in the sense that it functions. None of it makes sense in the sense that it should be here.

There is no noise except the lights. The phones are on the desks. The phones do not ring. You pick one up and there is a dial tone. You do not know who you would call. You put it down. The dial tone was not a dial tone. The dial tone had a rhythm that dials tones do not usually have.
