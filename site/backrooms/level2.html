PIPE DREAMS

you were warned. you came anyway. that's fine.

The note said not to come here. You came here. Level 2 is pipes. Not the manageable pipes of Level 1 — pipes everywhere. Pipes along the ceiling, pipes along the floor, pipes running through what used to be walls. The sound of water and something else moving through them never stops.

The lights here are emergency lights only. Red-tinted. There are long sections with no lights at all. Your eyes adjust. Then something moves in the part they've adjusted to, and you realize adjustment was the wrong goal.

⚠ SCRATCHING DETECTED — WALL TO YOUR LEFT
The sound moves downward. Then stops at floor level. Then there is silence. Then the silence is worse than the sound was.

Entity: Scratchers

Scratchers are the primary entity of Level 2. They make themselves known by dragging their claws along walls. You will hear them before you see them. Most survivors report that seeing them is not an improvement. Their behavior is territorial. Do not scratch the walls back. This was tried. It escalated the situation.
