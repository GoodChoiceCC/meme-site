The Museum of Absent Things houses the world's most comprehensive collection of things that are not here. Each exhibit has been carefully curated to represent something missing. Photography is permitted, but you will not capture what is absent.

The Last Thought Before Sleep

Whatever it was, it's gone now. This exhibit contains the exact thought you had last night as you lost consciousness. The museum has chosen not to label what it was.

The Concept of Monday

Monday has been placed in the collection as a concept. The chair faces the wall because Monday has always faced the wall. This is not a metaphor. This is just how Monday is.

John Pork's Final Call

A phone exhibit. The voicemail nobody listened to. The museum has received 47 visitor reports of hearing the phone ring during gallery hours. There is no phone in the exhibit. There has never been a phone in the exhibit.
